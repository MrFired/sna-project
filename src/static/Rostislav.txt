Attention all Trailblazers! HoyoClub is thrilled to introduce you to the latest addition to the Honkai series, Honkai: Star Rail!

This exciting spinoff, blending characters from Honkai Impact 3rd and gameplay elements from Genshin Impact. Join our club and embark on an incredible journey through the cosmos with Honkai: Star Rail.

All aboard the Astral Express, and let's explore the vast reaches of the Honkai universe together!"
